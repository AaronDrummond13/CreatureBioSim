# Ideas / future direction

## Overall feel
- **Spore cell stage–like experience:** Single-cell / simple-creature phase with evolution toward more complex forms.

## Food chain
- **Predator–prey:** Creature auto-seeks smaller creatures that move randomly. Once eaten, the spine can grow. Becomes a food chain simulation.
- **Herbivores:** Collect free-floating plant cells; distinct head/mouth from carnivores.
- **Carnivores:** Eat herbivores (and possibly other carnivores by size).

## World
- **Infinite generative world:** Procedural, unbounded space rather than a fixed arena.
- **Visible but non-interactive “beyond”:** Larger creatures / progression visible in the distance, blurred and out of reach (scope hint, not yet playable).

## Creature progression
- **Size progression:** Not only spine length — body mass, segment width, overall scale.
- **Eyes:** Can increase viewpoint (e.g. wider or longer sight range / cone).

## Systems
- **Traits / components / breeding:** Customisation and heredity (traits, modular parts, mating/offspring).
- **Editor:** Creature editor (e.g. Spore-style) for building and tuning creatures, traits, and components.
